CMU and Previous Experience

My main experience working with design is through implementing provided designs/mockups into a website.

Prior to CMU, I worked at Sherwin-Williams where I completed multiple website redesigns. The biggest being the careers website, which was my first lead role in a redesign. I worked directly with marketing and our UX/UI team to implement designs. This included responsive design, animations, necessary brand styling, and technical updates. This is where my interest in the design side of web development started and what inspired me to move into front-end specific work.

Once joining CMU, I was tasked with the ApplyGrad redesign where I implemented designs from student studies out of HCII. While ApplyGrad is no longer being updated due to a decision to use Slate instead, I've repurposed the design considerations from that and have been applying them to the GS Audit application. You can view the style guide, wireframes, and prototypes for that below.
